update_grid_state(grid: ndarray) → ndarray[source]ÔÉÅ

Creates the next grid from a given grid using the rules of the game: 1. Any dead cell with exactly 3 living neighbors becomes a living cell. 2. Any living cell with 2 or 3 living neighbors stays alive, otherwise it dies.

ParametersÔÉÅ

grid: np.ndarray

Numpy array containing 0s and 1s corresponding to dead cells and alive cells.

ReturnsÔÉÅ

updated_grid: np.ndarray

Numpy array containing 0s and 1s corresponding to dead cells and alive cells, after update.
